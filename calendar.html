Services

Typical Community Services & Seasonal Events

Texas Township provides these recurring services throughout the year. Exact dates and sign-ups are announced on the official township website.

Bulk Trash Pickup

Spring curbside collection for large household items not accepted in regular trash.

Electronics Recycling

Seasonal drop-off events for TVs, computers, and other e-waste items.

Leaf & Yard Waste Collection

Curbside pickup of leaves and yard debris in fall and spring seasons.

Summer Community Events

Parks programming, farmers market, and local gatherings throughout the summer.

Election Days

State, county, and local elections held at designated Texas Township polling locations.

Public Hearings

Zoning, planning, and ordinance review hearings open to all township residents.
