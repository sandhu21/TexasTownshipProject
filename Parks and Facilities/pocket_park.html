Pocket Park

Discover a hidden gem in the heart of Texas Corners.

Address

7110 W Q Ave, Kalamazoo, MI 49009

Features

  • Benches and seating areas
  • Playground
  • Small rock wall
  • Public Restrooms

Description

Enjoy a small and enjoyable park located in the center of Texas Corners where young children can play safely. Pocket Park is located right behind the Texas Corners Farmers Market and the fire station.
