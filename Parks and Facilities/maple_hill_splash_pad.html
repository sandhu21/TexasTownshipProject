Maple Hill Splash Pad

Cool off and have fun at the Maple Hill Splash Pad!

About Maple Hill Splash Pad

Address

6603 Texas Dr, Kalamazoo, MI 49009

Features

  • Interactive Water Features
  • Sprayers and Fountains
  • Adjacent Playground
  • Picnic Areas
  • Restroom Facilities
  • Multi-use Fields

Description

A vibrant, child-friendly splash pad offering cooling fun during hot summer days. Admission is free. Maple Hill Splash Pad re-opens on Memorial Day each year.

Summer Fun for the Whole Family

Maple Hill Splash Pad is one of Texas Corners' most beloved warm-weather destinations -- a free, interactive water play area where children can run through jets, sprays, and splash features in a safe, supervised environment. As one of the few splash pad facilities in the area, it draws families from throughout Texas Township and beyond during the summer months.

The facility is well-maintained, thoughtfully designed for different age groups, and completely free to use -- making it an accessible summertime staple for every family in the community regardless of budget. On a hot Michigan summer day, there's no better place in Texas Corners to spend an afternoon.

Water PlayInteractive splash features, jets, and spray elements designed for children of all ages
