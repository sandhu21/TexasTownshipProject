Texas Township Trailway

Location: Starts at 6th St and runs through Texas Township

Features:

  • Paved multi-use trail
  • Suitable for walking, jogging, and cycling
  • Scenic natural surroundings
  • Connects various parts of the township
  • Parking available at trailheads

Description: A beautiful and convenient trailway that offers a great opportunity for outdoor recreation and community connectivity.
