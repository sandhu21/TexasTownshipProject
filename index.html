    About the Township

    A community worth
    exploring.

    Texas Corners is a vibrant unincorporated community at the heart of Texas Township in Kalamazoo County. Surrounded by nature preserves, connected by trailways, and filled with locally owned businesses — it's Southwest Michigan living at its finest.

    From weekend farmers markets to award-winning craft breweries, every corner of this township offers something to discover.

    Al Sabo Land Preserve Over 800 acres of protected natural land with miles of trails
    Local First 31 unique local spots — restaurants, boutiques, and services
    Kalamazoo County Part of one of Michigan's most livable and connected counties
