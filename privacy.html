Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

This website does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information simply to browse. We do not collect your name, address, phone number, or any other personal data unless you voluntarily contact us.

The only personal information we may receive is an email address, and only when you choose to reach out to us directly by sending an email to texascornerscontact@gmail.com. This contact method is entirely voluntary and initiated by you.

Third-Party Services

To deliver a high-quality experience, this website loads resources from the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy and data practices that are independent of this site:

  • Bootstrap CDN (jsDelivr) — Provides the CSS and JavaScript framework. jsDelivr may log request metadata such as IP address and browser type. See the jsDelivr Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — Loads the Outfit and Playfair Display typefaces. Google may collect data about font requests. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome CDN (Cloudflare) — Provides icon assets. Cloudflare may process request metadata. See the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps Embeds — Some pages embed Google Maps iframes to show location information. When these embeds load, Google may collect data according to its own privacy terms. See the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not control the data practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their respective policies if you have concerns.

Cookies

This website itself does not set any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. We do not use tools like Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other behavioral tracking software.

Third-party services embedded on this site (such as Google Maps or Google Fonts) may set their own cookies in accordance with their privacy policies. Your browser settings allow you to manage or block cookies from any domain.

How We Use Your Information

If you contact us by email, we use your email address solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add your address to any mailing list, share it with third parties, or use it for any marketing purpose without your explicit consent.

We have no automated data collection systems, no user accounts, and no advertising partnerships that would involve processing your personal data.

Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained only as long as is reasonably necessary to address your enquiry and maintain an appropriate record of communications. We do not archive or store email data in any persistent database separate from our email provider.

Your Rights

Because we collect minimal personal data, your practical rights in connection with this site are straightforward. You may:

  • Choose not to contact us — in which case we hold no personal data about you at all.
  • Request that we delete any email correspondence you have sent us, by emailing texascornerscontact@gmail.com.
  • Request confirmation of what data (if any) we hold about you.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) or the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We are committed to honoring reasonable data rights requests.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately at texascornerscontact@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

External Links

Pages on this site may contain links to external websites, including the official websites of local businesses, parks, and government agencies. Once you leave this site, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the bottom of this page. Continued use of this website after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the data practices of this website, please reach out to us:

  • Email: texascornerscontact@gmail.com

We will respond to all reasonable privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
