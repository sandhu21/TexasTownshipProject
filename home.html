About Texas Township

Learn more about our thriving community

A Brief History

Texas Township was founded in the late 19th century by a group of settlers who were drawn to the area's fertile land and scenic landscapes. Over the decades, the township has grown into a vibrant and diverse community, known for its rich cultural heritage and strong sense of community spirit.

Our Vision

At the heart of Texas Township's vision is a commitment to sustainable growth, community engagement, and the preservation of our natural resources. We strive to create a thriving environment where residents can live, work, and play, while also fostering a strong, locally-oriented economy.

Parks & Recreation

From scenic hiking trails to well-maintained sports facilities, Texas Township offers a wealth of opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. Enjoy the great outdoors and connect with your community.

Local Events

Throughout the year, Texas Township hosts a variety of community events that bring residents together, celebrate our rich heritage, and showcase the talents of local artists and performers. Join us for festivals, concerts, and more!

Civic Engagement

Texas Township is proud to have a highly engaged and civic-minded population. Residents are actively involved in local government, volunteer organizations, and community initiatives that shape the future of our township.
